Real-time water level time series forecasts for 84 hours are provided every 6 hours daily for the AHPS/NOAA monitoring stations.

Model results are presented in green color titled iFlood_Mason_ForecastModel_Forcing and are compared with NOAA official forecast models Extratropical Surge and Tide Operational Forecast System (ESTOFS) and Extratropical Storm Surge Model (ETSS). Both NOAA operational forecast model uses Global Forecast System (GFS) meteorological forcing to predict water levels.
