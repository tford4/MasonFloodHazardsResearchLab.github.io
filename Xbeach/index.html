The Mason Flood Hazards Research Lab has been studying the storm surge and waves attenuation by the marshes of Magothy Bay Natural Area Preserve for the last 3 years. Our group has extensively monitoring the hydrodynamic regime in the marshes and documented several hurricanes and storms.
